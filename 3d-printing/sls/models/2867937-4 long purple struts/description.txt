These are struts to supplement a Zometool kit. Zometool (http://zometool.com) provides struts in four different directions (orbits) relative to the icosahedral symmetry group of the Zome ball. An infinite number of other directions are possible. &quot;Purple&quot; is a direction defined in vZome (http://vzome.com), a software application for designing Zome models.<br /> <br /> &nbsp;
